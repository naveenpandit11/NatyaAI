How It Works
01
Real-time Capture

Webcam streams live at 2 fps. Each frame is encoded and dispatched to the AI backend for analysis.

02
Landmark Extraction

MediaPipe detects 21 hand landmarks per hand — 84 coordinates total — encoding joint positions precisely.

03
Classification

A Random Forest model trained on all 19 mudras predicts the gesture and returns a probability score in milliseconds.
